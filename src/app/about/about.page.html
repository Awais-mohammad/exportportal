ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE

Exportportal.pk is an online B2B platform aiming to be a comprehensive international marketplace specifically designed for Pakistani exporters , which may include Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) and their counterparts covering all sectors and industries. h

WHY TO CHOOSE US

The vision is to provide such a unified and comprehensive platform where all the companies in Pakistan, which are exporters today and may aim to become exporters in future, are listed. Such a platform will become a directory for these companies. It will help them project their profiles to the international market. The companies will be listed and categorized according to their products and services. Such a categorization will facilitate prioritization, transparency, and ease-of-use for the end user. Some of the key features for listed companies are:
● All sorts of companies will be listed on the portal, e.g. food, merchandise, IT.
● The information such as detailed brand information, company description, contact.details and products will be provided on the company specific page.
● Each company will have a dedicated page for its brand.
● Based on the membership status companies could list and add products with details
● Top exporters based on certain criterias, certifications and ratings will be promoted.
● Our team will be doing active marketing of the brands on social media and other internet marketplaces.

WHAT IS EXPORTPORTAL

A country like Pakistan with such a large population, famous for its diverse industries with a quality products portfolio and continuously growing businesses, offers great opportunities for international traders. In contrast, Pakistani exporters still have very little penetration and presence in the e-commerce domain. Many companies do not even have an online marketplace or presence, which limits the opportunities and recognition for them. Industries that are located in remote areas have little to poor internet connectivity. Several SMEs and micro export organizations are doing business in decades old traditional ways such as contact based trading. To exploit the full potential of such a dynamic market a digital platform will serve as backbone for Pakistan’s trading and exports.
